Angelik Laboy

Is a Puerto Rican director, writer, and editor, currently based in Atlanta, Georgia. From an early age, she started to produce and write several adaptations for the Boys and Girls Clubâ€™s theater production and was selected to inaugurate her play Juguetes Deliantes in spring 2008. It is then through directing/editing several student films that she found her passion within filmmaking. In 2016, Laboy created two productions called 3 Eyes Production and Crazy=Genius(7x) to increase the creation of films within schools and in Puerto Rico. The projects led her to win Best Director in Newark IFF Youth Festival, and inaugural in Pequeno Cineasta (Best Writer and Best PSA), Vex Robotics Online Challenges, and STEM Voice Competition (National Group Winners). Currently in the process of receiving her B.S in Computer Science and Film Studies minor at Georgia Institute of Technology. Here, she co-founded Filmmakers @ GT, a student organization aimed to create short films and build a community of film-lovers, where she leads as Vice President and Head Director.
